COLLEGE Pre-Registration

Welcome to Sacred Heart School of Cavite, Inc - Recoletos de Alfonso’s Online Registration. Please read the Registration Procedure carefully before submitting your application. Thank you very much!

Registration Procedure

1. Fill up the Pre-registration form
2. Check your email for your temporary username and password
3. After signing in, please fill up the student’s information sheet
4. For new students, upload available requirements (you may skip this page if requirements are not yet available)
5. Upload your picture (colored picture with white background)
6. Check your email for registration confirmation and other instructions
